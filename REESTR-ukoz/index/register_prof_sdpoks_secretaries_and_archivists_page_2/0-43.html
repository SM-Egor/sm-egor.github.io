REGISTER PROF-SDPOKS SECRETARIES AND ARCHIVISTS (page 2)

Register of professionals and specialists in the field of secretariat is commercial in nature and developed edited administration portal site based on personal recommendations of regional teachers, students and employers. We hope that this register will be useful for those who are looking for a job in the office of the Secretariat, as well as employers who require an employee with expertise in the theory of secure storage, security, sharing, structuring and adaptation of electronic information.

If you can recommend to colleagues in this register, please contact the regional coordinator of the portal registry. We with attention and gratitude consider all constructive comments and suggestions regarding the display of information in the registry.

With the aim of providing the most convenient information to the employer - the site menu, the general introductory information, instructions, as well as personal data of applicants informative portal registry SDPOKS are in Ukrainian, English and Russian languages.

Dear employers!

If you need a secretary, clerk or archivist with professional knowledge of the theory of secure storage, protection, sharing, structuring and adaptation of electronic information - ask the applicant to the post-№IND SDPOKS assigned to each entrant SDPOKS registry, as a confirmation of at his knowledge of the above.

Dear entrants registry!

(New) Only applicants registry. If, at the moment, you have not employed and are guaranteed interested in finding a job as secretary, clerk or archivist and consent to the release of their informative data to a prospective employer to consider your candidacy for the above positions or analog - send us an your CV to e-mail:

resume.reestr-sdpoks@ukr.net
