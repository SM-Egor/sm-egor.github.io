SDPOKS - common core of basic concepts...

Theory SDPOKS (abreviation) - professional service computer system based on the theory of safe storage, protection, sharing, system structure and adapt electronic information (including TOR and VPN, as well as options for domestic TOP phishing etc..). Emphasis on leakage competitive Charting company information.

Unlike conventional Secretary deloproizvolitelya or archivist, secretary-clerk or archivist who has knowledge of the theory SDPOKS, alone and in its entirety, is able to control the workplace and computer system for monitoring and leak competitive Charting company information.

It is no secret that in our time, electronic espionage is gaining strong momentum. Competition between companies and firms makes the last to give priority to conceal competitive component of information, ie the information that may be of interest to competitors.

Carrier almost full volume to the company, as a rule, the secretary or secretary-clerk, who has access to the entire turnover of company records.

The company's management wants to be confident in the fact that an employee who holds the position of Secretary delopoizvoditelya or archivist of the company is able to fully control their workplace (including the work of the computer system with administration rights) and will not allow leakage of closed internal company information that can bring serious and sometimes irreparable damage to the company.
