We do not want to prevent anyone from creating groups and pages on Facebook or any other platform about our courses or SDPOKS in general. However, we do ask the relevant parties to respect our intellectual property rights by ensuring that the pages and groups do not use our logo or create the impression that they originate from or are approved by us.
