SPECIFICATION OF REGISTRATION IN THE REGISTER SDPOKS

Please note that in connection with independence in the political-military-conjunctural component of the provision of information, as well as for safe and smooth operation of the portal registry 24/7 - Technical data center portal server registry listed and registered in the British Virgin islands. These applicants registry available to the employer around the clock for the entire calendar week... ⇒

For registration in the registry SDPOKS, you should contact the training organization in which you have completed courses theory SDPOKS or directly to your private teacher theory SDPOKS, asking you to register in the register SDPOKS to display your informative data by assigning you a personal №IND-SDPOKS with status - a professional or expert, respectively. Registration in the registry is free.

If you have not studied the theory SDPOKS, you need to learn appropriate educational material and protect the status - professional or skilled in the art. Ads for courses on the theory SDPOKS, schools and private teachers are usually self-published on the Internet and other advertising media.

Depending on the method of studying the theory of educational material (remote, lecture, lecture remotely), as well as system and urgency of training, the average study periods of teaching material theory - orietirovochno 7-20 days, the average cost of education - from 300 UAH.

CAUTION Before you pay for tuition, be sure to inform the Registry Coordinator e-mail the teacher, who plan to undergo theory SDPOKS and wait for an answer. Tuition is highly desirable to produce a personalized payment card of your teacher (we recommend Privat). Be sure to retain the original receipt of payment for tuition. If you are unable to find a teacher on their own or have any questions in this area - write registry coordinator.
